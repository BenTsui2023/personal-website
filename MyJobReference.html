My Job Reference

My Working experience

Working as a building services engineer for four years, I was fortunately participated into three big construction projects. They are Hong Kong Children Hospital, CUHK Medical Centre and North Lantau Hospital Hong Kong Infection Control Centre. I learned a lot and was equipped with strong technical skills on electrical system in a building.

Most importantly, I learned how to work collaboratively with cross-functional teams such as HVAC enginner, fire services energinner and civil enginner etc. I used to keep my team coordinated and connected do better work. Also, having experience in running big project, I am able to have multiple tasks in progress at the same time and effectively manage the stress and workload.
