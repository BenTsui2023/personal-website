My Second Hobby

When i have free time, I usually listen to music and play guitar to release my pressure. The music genres i love are metal and rock. The following list introduces the type of metal and rock music i love:

  1. Power Metal
    • Originating in the mid 1980’s
    • A mixture of classic metal with speed metal
    • Usually using keyboards to create symphonic element
    • Usually having fantasy melodic sound to create dramatic and powerful sound
  2. Progressive Metal
    • Originating in the mid 1980’s
    • A mix of classic heavy metal with progressive rock
    • Usually inspired by differenet types of music such as Jazz and classical music to create some innovative fusion music
  3. Thrash Metal
    • Originating in the early 1980’s
    • Aggression and often fast tempo
    • Usually having a fast, complex and technical guitar solo and double bass drumming
  4. Rock
    • Originating in the late 1940’s
    • Developed from blues and rhythm and blues music
    • The lyrics have lyrics to express social concerns and life styles
  5. Math Rock
    • Originating in the late 1980’s
    • Having complex rhythmic structures
    • Using non-standard and frequently changing time signatures
    • Usually dominated by guitars and drums, not the vocal
  6. Post Rock
    • Originating in the late 1980’s
    • Often instrumental,vocals are often omitted
    • Usually using repetition of riff and subtle change with dynamics to build up emotional change in music
